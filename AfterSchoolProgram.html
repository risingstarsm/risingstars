After School Program


Our after school program is for children ages between 5 to 13 years. Our after school hours are from 3:15 PM to 6:00 PM (Mon/Tues/Thurs/Fri) and 1:15 PM to 6:00 PM (Wed).

* Our school follows Lake Washington School District. In case the school district announces closure for natural calamities e.g. snow days, our school will also remain closed.

* The last day of Rosa Parks will be the last day of After School Program. Summer Program enrollment is separate.

Pickup service: We pickup from Rosa Parks Elementary.

Snack: Kids get healthy snack on arrival.

Homework support: Kids get support to finish their school homework, Kumon/Bestin work.

Indoor activities: Kids are free to choose activities from art and craft, STEM games, board games, reading books, practicing writing skills etc.

Outdoor activities: Kids get outdoor recess time.
