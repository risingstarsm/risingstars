Half Day Program

Our half-day program is for children ages between 3 years to 6 years old starting from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. We provide light morning snack. Lunch is not provided in school.

Our curriculum includes:

Language Skills: We teach alphabets recognition, phonics, site words, blending sounds. Kids are also introduced to reading, writing, simple to complex sentence development and better verbal communication.

Math Skills : We teach counting numbers, counting backward/forward, skipping, number pattern, series, sequence, addition, subtraction, multiplication and all the math concepts for beginners.

Science : We encourage curiosity, thus provide learning through practical activities and simple experiments in our class activities. Kids observe life outside like bugs, butterflies, plants, soil etc and we document their experience and learning.

Colors: Kids learns primary colors, secondary colors, color mixing and creating new colors.

Shapes and geometry: We teach different shapes, patterns and introduce our kids to basic geometry.

Arts Craft and Cultural activities: We design activities based on class current theme and seasons. Kids learn about different world cultures and their celebration.
