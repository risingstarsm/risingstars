Welcome to Rising Stars Montessori

Unlocking curiosity and knowledge

Rising Stars Montessori and after school program is a licensed home based preschool and afterschool. We are focussed on learning, creativity and overall social, emotional and physical development of kids in a fun filled environment.

We have age appropriate curriculum for Montessori and After School Program. In our school children get a safe and nurturing environment with a schedule that best meet their needs. Each child is treated with respect as a valuable individual. Personal attention is given to every child.

Owner/Director
